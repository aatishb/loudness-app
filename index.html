Loudness Curve Generator

Investigating the perceptual nature of loudness

In this interactive, you can examine how our perception of loudness varies with the frequency of the tone. You can compare the loudness of a set of notes to that of a fixed reference.

If the perceived loudness of a tone were independent its frequency, then all the notes should sound match the loudness of the reference note at the same volume level. Using this tool, you can investigate the dependence between loudness and frequency.

Caution: Notes with different pitches can have *very* different loudnesses, and if you jump from a low pitch to a much higher pitch, you might end up hearing a painfully loud sound. To avoid this, we recommend moving up or down one note at a time (i.e. only switch to nearby notes), and when adjusting the loudness, work your way up very gradually (i.e. don't move the slider all the way to the right).
